About Me

Hi, this is Ke Ren from China. I'm studying Interactive Media Design in Alqonquin college. I am fascinated by the history and culture of each city and have worked in this field for several years. I hope that in the future, I will bring the history and culture of a country to people's life by digital means.

I used to be a 3D modeler for over 15 years, and direct and build art teams to create emotive, immersive and dynamic 3D content for VR and BIM. I like drawing, programming and soccer. I expect to improve myself and make more friends in Algonquin. Welcome to my website!
